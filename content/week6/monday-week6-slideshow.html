Jesus' Purposeful Daily Life

Even the Son of God had daily rhythms!

  • Early morning prayer (Mark 1:35)
  • Teaching in the day (Luke 19:47)
  • Evening fellowship (Luke 24:29)
  • Regular Sabbath observance (Luke 4:16)
  • Withdrawing to solitary places (Luke 5:16)

If Jesus needed rhythms, so do we!

Sabbath: Sustaining Your Purpose

Rest is not laziness - it's obedience!

Sabbath Essentials:

  • Complete rest from work
  • Worship and celebration
  • Family and community
  • Reflection on God's goodness
  • Preparation for the week ahead

You can't pour from an empty cup!

Avoiding Purpose Burnout

Warning Signs

  • Constant exhaustion
  • Loss of joy
  • Spiritual dryness
  • Relational strain
  • Physical symptoms

Prevention Plan

  • Daily quiet time
  • Weekly Sabbath
  • Monthly retreat
  • Quarterly review
  • Annual sabbatical
