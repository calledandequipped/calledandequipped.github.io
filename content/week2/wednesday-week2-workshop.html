Guided Listening Prayer Workshop

Learning to Distinguish and Trust God's Voice

Week 2, Day 3: Cultivating a Listening Heart

Workshop Purpose: Through guided exercises, you'll practice listening to God, learn to distinguish His voice from others, and develop confidence in what you hear. This is a safe space to practice and grow.

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Your Information

Opening: Preparing Your Heart

Before We Begin:

"Be still before the Lord and wait patiently for him."
- Psalm 37:7

Session 1: Quieting the Noise (10 minutes)

10min
"Lord, I give You all the noise in my mind..."

Now mentally place all these concerns in God's hands

Session 2: Recognizing Different Voices (15 minutes)

15min

Now listen for 5 minutes, then categorize what you hear:

God's Voice

Peaceful, loving, aligns with Scripture

My Voice

My desires, fears, or reasoning

Other Voices

Condemnation, confusion, pressure

Session 3: The Divine Echo Test (10 minutes)

10min

🔊 The Divine Echo Test

God often confirms His voice through multiple sources. Test what you heard:

Echo 1: Scripture

Does it align with God's Word?

Echo 2: Peace

Do you sense God's peace about it?

Echo 3: Wise Counsel

Would mature believers confirm this?

Echo 4: Fruit

Will it produce good fruit?

Session 4: Extended Listening Prayer (15 minutes)

15min

Focus Question: "Lord, what is one thing You want me to know about my calling today?"

Listen without forcing. Be patient. God may speak through:

  • A gentle impression or thought
  • A scripture that comes to mind
  • A picture or image
  • A sense of knowing
  • An emotion or feeling
