Confirming Your Calling

Developing Spiritual Discernment for Direction

Week 2, Friday - Session 6

"Let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts..."

Colossians 3:15 (NIV)

Is This Really God?

Biblical tests for authentic calling

  • God confirms His direction in multiple ways
  • Confirmation protects us from presumption
  • Authentic callings withstand testing
  • God's timing is part of His confirmation

The Peace of Christ

"And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."

- Philippians 4:7

  • Peace acts as an "umpire" in decisions
  • Not absence of conflict, but inner assurance
  • Distinguish peace from comfort or convenience
  • When peace is absent, pause and seek God

Alignment with Scripture

The Non-Negotiable Test

  • God never contradicts His Word
  • Study principles, not just isolated verses
  • Consider the full counsel of Scripture
  • Beware of "proof-texting" to justify desires

"Your word is a lamp for my feet, a light on my path." - Psalm 119:105

Confirmation Through Community

"Plans fail for lack of counsel, but with many advisers they succeed."

- Proverbs 15:22

  • Seek mature, spiritually discerning advisors
  • Value diverse perspectives
  • Be open to correction
  • Look for consistent confirmation

Open and Closed Doors

Open Doors

Divine opportunities
Favor and provision
Alignment of resources
God-initiated connections

Closed Doors

Divine protection
Timing issues
Need for preparation
Redirection to better paths

"I will open doors no one can shut, and shut doors no one can open" - Revelation 3:7

Prophetic Encouragement

"Do not treat prophecies with contempt but test them all; hold on to what is good"

- 1 Thessalonians 5:20-21

  • Prophetic words should confirm, not initiate direction
  • Must align with Scripture and inner witness
  • Consider the source and character of the messenger
  • Look for multiple confirmations over time

Calling Confirmation Checklist

1. Scriptural Alignment

Does this direction align with biblical principles?

2. Peace of Christ

Do I have God's peace about this decision?

3. Wise Counsel

What do mature believers discern about this?

4. Circumstantial Evidence

Are doors opening or closing in confirmation?

5. Personal Witness

Does the Holy Spirit confirm this internally?
