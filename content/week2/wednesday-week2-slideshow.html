The Samuel Model

Learning to recognize God's voice

  • Samuel heard God but didn't recognize Him at first
  • He needed Eli's guidance to understand
  • Three calls: God is patient with our learning
  • Posture of surrender: "Your servant is listening"

Creating Space for Silence

"Very early in the morning, while it was still dark, Jesus got up, left the house and went off to a solitary place, where he prayed."

- Mark 1:35

  • Designate a sacred space for listening
  • Start with 10 minutes of silence daily
  • Remove digital distractions
  • Practice "Sabbath moments" throughout the day

Distinguishing God's Voice

Characteristics of God's voice:

  • Aligns with Scripture
  • Brings peace (Colossians 3:15)
  • Exalts Christ (John 16:14)
  • Produces life and freedom
  • Often gentle and persistent

The Role of Fasting

"While they were worshiping the Lord and fasting, the Holy Spirit said..."

- Acts 13:2

  • Fasting sharpens spiritual senses
  • Creates space for God to speak
  • Demonstrates spiritual hunger
  • Types: Full, partial, media, Daniel fast

Guided Listening Prayer

1. Prepare

Quiet your heart and mind

2. Invite

Ask the Holy Spirit to speak

3. Listen

Wait in silence (5-10 minutes)

4. Journal

Record impressions, scriptures, pictures

Practice this daily this week

The 24-Hour Media Fast

Your Challenge:

  1. Choose a 24-hour period this week
  2. Disconnect from:
    • Social media
    • News outlets
    • Entertainment streaming
  3. Replace with intentional spiritual practices
  4. Journal what God reveals in the silence
