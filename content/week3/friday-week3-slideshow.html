Stewardship of Talents

Multiplying Your God-Given Gifts

Week 3, Friday - Session 9

"For it will be like a man going on a journey, who called his servants and entrusted to them his property. To one he gave five talents, to another two, to another one, to each according to his ability."

Matthew 25:14-15 (ESV)

Understanding the Parable

Jesus teaches us profound truths about stewardship:

  • God entrusts each of us with unique gifts
  • Our talents vary, but all are valuable
  • We're accountable for how we use them
  • Fear shouldn't paralyze our potential
  • Faithfulness in small things leads to greater opportunities

  • People notice when Christians excel

Modern-Day Talent Multipliers

Sarah's Story: From One Talent to Ten

Sarah discovered her gift for organizing while arranging her church's book club. She developed this talent through online courses and practice. Today, she runs a professional organizing business that tithes 20% to missions and employs three single mothers from her church.

Marcus's Journey: Tech Skills for the Kingdom

A software developer who thought his skills were "just secular," Marcus began creating apps for ministries. His free church management app now serves over 500 congregations worldwide.

Breaking the Burial Mindset

Why We Bury Our Talents:

  • Fear of failure or criticism
  • Comparing ourselves to others
  • Perfectionism paralysis
  • False humility
  • Waiting for "perfect" conditions

Remember: God doesn't call the qualified, He qualifies the called!
