8 / 23

Elements of Nehemiah's Holy Ambition

  • Saw a genuine need (broken walls)
  • Felt personal burden (wept and mourned)
  • Sought God first (fasted and prayed)
  • Used his position wisely (cupbearer to king)
  • Took calculated risks (asked for leave)
  • Mobilized others (rallied the people)
  • Persevered through opposition

Defining Holy Ambition

Holy Ambition is:

A God-given passion to meet a God-sized need

for God's glory, not our own

10 / 23

Holy Ambition vs. Selfish Ambition

Holy Ambition

  • God-centered
  • Others-focused
  • Kingdom advancement
  • Sacrificial
  • Brings unity
  • Produces lasting fruit

Selfish Ambition

  • Self-centered
  • Personal gain
  • Platform building
  • Self-protective
  • Creates division
  • Temporary success
