Your Transformative 6-Week Journey

Three powerful sessions each week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) combining biblical teaching, practical workshops, and spiritual exercises.

Week 1

Created for a Purpose

Discover your divine design through Ephesians 2:10, understand your spiritual DNA, and learn from Joseph's journey to purpose. Complete spiritual gifts assessments and create your Divine Design Profile.

Week 2

Hearing God's Voice

Develop spiritual discernment through biblical examples, practice Lectio Divina, and learn to distinguish God's voice. Includes 24-hour media fast and creating your Calling Confirmation Checklist.

Week 3

Aligning Passion with Purpose

Where deep gladness meets the world's need. Study Nehemiah's holy ambition, develop marketplace ministry strategies, and create your talent multiplication plan based on Matthew 25.

Week 4

Overcoming Purpose Blockers

Break through fear and doubt with Joshua 1:9, conquer comparison, and learn patience in God's process. Includes deliverance sessions, faith declarations, and social media detox.

Week 5

Practical Purpose Implementation

Move from revelation to manifestation with strategic planning, building support systems, and Kingdom financial principles. Create your 90-day action plan and purpose funding strategy.

Week 6

Living Your Purpose Daily

Sustain a purpose-driven life with daily rhythms, legacy building, and multiplication. Craft your mission statement, create legacy vision board, and participate in virtual commissioning service.
